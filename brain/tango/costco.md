## 3-Pack Proposal

### Product Format

**Format:** 3 √ó 8oz squeeze bottles ‚Äî Hot, Sriracha, Truffle
**Retail price:** $19.00

**Why this combo:**
- **Hot** ‚Äî OG best seller, always #1
- **Sriracha** ‚Äî Cheapest COGS ($2.74), clean Huy Fong alternative, massive category
- **Truffle** ‚Äî Premium differentiator, $17 standalone on Amazon, best TRUFF competitor on market
- Sriracha and truffle are two of the most popular hot sauce flavors of all time ‚Äî broad appeal in a Costco demo environment

**Why $19 is genius:**
- Truffle alone retails for $17 on Amazon
- Customer gets 3 full-size bottles for $19 = insane perceived value
- Trial machine ‚Äî they taste 3 flavors, become repeat buyers across all channels
